About SpurBuddy

SpurBuddy is a startup studio dedicated to building extraordinary companies from emerging markets.

Together with innovative partners we breed startups that create value ultimately contributing to the common good.

STARTUP

We help in leveraging technologies and capitals to build digital ecosystems in high growth sectors by developing customer-centric and value-added services for African customers, enabling African SMEs to scale their enterprises, while creating jobs and empowerment opportunities for everyone.

COPORATE INNOVATION

We assist organizations to sustain growth and business improvements over a continuous and consistent basis. We also build new product, feature, operational process or a business model. with the help of our creative workers we execute execute creativity and optimize business processes.
